Changing how the world uses social media

Here at Valence, we have become fed up with society’s trend away from real human interaction as technology dominates the ways in which we communicate. Valence is the new social network to put an end to social networks. By allowing people to find others within a close radius who are open to talk, Valence aims to push nearby strangers to connect with each other and revive the lost art of face-to-face conversation.

Real Connections

Valence does not allow users to hide behind their screens and interact through meaningless "likes" or "pokes". Our app simply connects users looking to talk in close proximity, and the rest is up to you!

Completely Free

Not only will Valence cost absolutely nothing, it will also be free of ads! Why not give it a try?

Easy to Use

By eliminating uneeded features, Valence is extremely easy to navigate. A click of a button is all it takes to sign up and meet new people.

Heres a glimpse of what Valence has to offer.

Sleek Design

While creating Valence, our designers focused on building an app with a clean, aesthetically pleasing layout. Our attention to detail allows users to navigate our app quickly and effortlessly-- getting you off your phone and engaged in conversation in no time!

Networking

Connections our users make are more personal than those created through other social media. By stripping our network down to the bare essentials and introducing users face-to-face, we aim to eliminate purposeless online interaction. With Valence, your next best friend, buisness partner, or mentor may be just a conversation away.
